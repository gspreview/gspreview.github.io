About us

Next leading company on big data.

7 Bridges Inc.

The Seven Bridges of Königsberg is a historically notable problem in mathematics. Its negative resolution by Leonhard Euler in 1736 laid the foundations of graph theory and prefigured the idea of topology.

7 Bridges Inc. was founded by the team that created GRAPE, an award-winning system for parallelizing graph computation. 7Bridges’ mission is to revolutionize the data landscape with an ease-of-use solution and increased performance by unifying AI and big data with graphs.
